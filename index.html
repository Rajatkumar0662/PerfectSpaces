How We Work

My Perfect Space Design offer a comprehensive design service, creating beautifully crafted interiors for discerning clients. At our work place in Bangalore,Karntaka the creative team is led by Our Best Designers. His background as a fashion and textile designer is reflected in her love of form, texture and colour.

Discovery Meeting

This is an initial opportunity to discuss the project in detail and take the brief. During the discovery meeting we learn about the client's personality, their style and their individual lifestyle, tastes and aspirations.

Concept Design

In response to the client's brief a concept scheme is created for discussion. This will be presented in the form of draft layouts showing space utilisation, any structural modifications. At this stage we produce a draft Cost Plan.

Detail Design

Once all elements in the concept design are agreed with the client, the project moves to the Detail Design and Specification stage. Samples are presented for discussion and client approval.

Procurement

Once the detail design has been agreed a detailed cost plan is presented.This sets out the cost of each individual element per room and a summarised project total. To give our clients best value, within the procurement service.

Project Management

The overall programme is drawn up and agreed with the client. Progress of the project is managed and monitored on site on a regular basis and any issues arising in the course of the project are resolved in a timely and pragmatic manner.

Dressing & Handover

With construction and decoration completed we arrange for a full site clean before installing window treatments and carpets. We then coordinate the delivery and positioning of all furniture and furnishings to create the finished look
